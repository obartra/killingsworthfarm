At Killingsworth Vineyard, we are dedicated to producing only the finest wines.

Our process begins in the vineyard, where we carefully tend to our grapes and ensure that they receive the best possible care. Once the grapes are harvested, they are taken to our state-of-the-art winery, where they are carefully fermented and aged. Our winemaking team then blends the finest varietals to create a range of distinctive, flavorful wines. From start to finish, every step of our process is guided by a commitment to quality and a passion for great wine. We believe that the care and attention we put into our wines is evident in every sip, and we are proud to share our passion with wine lovers everywhere.

  • 1. Harvesting
  • 2.  Fermentation
  • 3. Aging
  • 4. Blending

  • Experience the art of hand-picking grapes at their peak of ripeness

    At Killingsworth Vineyard, the harvesting process is one of the most important steps in creating great wine. Our grapes are hand-picked at the peak of ripeness to ensure that they have the fullest flavor and aroma. We use only the finest grapes in our wines, and the harvesting process is key to ensuring that they are of the highest quality.

    Our experienced harvesters work tirelessly to ensure that each grape is handled with care, and that only the best are used in our wines. We believe that a great wine starts with the best grapes, and our harvesting process is an essential part of that. Come and see for yourself the care and attention to detail that goes into each and every bottle of wine at Killingsworth Vineyard.

  • Watch as grapes are transformed into rich, complex wines

    Once the grapes have been harvested, they are taken to our winery for fermentation. This is the process where the sugars in the grapes are converted into alcohol and carbon dioxide. Our winemaking team carefully monitors the fermentation process, adjusting the temperature and other variables to ensure that the grapes are fermented to perfection.

    The result is a wine with a rich, complex flavor and a smooth, balanced finish. The careful attention to detail and precision during the fermentation process sets our wines apart and ensures that each bottle is of the highest quality. We believe that great wine starts with the best grapes and ends with a precise fermentation process. Come and taste the difference for yourself at Killingsworth Vineyard.

  • Savor the flavor and aroma of wine that has been perfectly aged to maturity.

    After fermentation, the wine is aged in oak barrels to allow it to develop its full flavor and aroma. The length of aging and the type of oak used will vary depending on the type of wine being produced. Our winemaking team carefully monitors the aging process, tasting the wine regularly to ensure that it is developing as expected.

    By the time the wine is bottled, it is ready to be enjoyed to its fullest. The aging process is a critical step in the creation of great wine, and at Killingsworth Vineyard, we take it very seriously. Come and taste the difference that a perfect aging process makes in our wines.

  • Enjoy the creativity and expertise of our winemaking team as they craft your next favorite wine.

    The final step in our process is blending. This is where our winemaking team carefully combines different varietals to create a range of distinctive, flavorful wines. We believe that blending is an art, and our winemakers are experts in creating the perfect blend of flavors and aromas.

    Whether you prefer a bold, full-bodied red or a crisp, refreshing white, our wines are sure to impress. The blending process brings our wines to life, and we are proud to offer a range of wines that are as unique and flavorful as they are beautiful. Come and taste the difference that a perfect blend makes in our wines at Killingsworth Vineyard.
